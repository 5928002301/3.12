Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute claass of poses and slow movement focuses on asana(proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to fosted your mind and body connection
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-syncronized movement - you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses
Vinyasa Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice is can be a perfect way to rehabilitate an injury
